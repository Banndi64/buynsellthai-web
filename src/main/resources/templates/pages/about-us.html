About Us

In 2019, Thailand faced a serious plastic and electronic waste problem. Landfills in Bangkok and other major cities were reaching capacity, and many reusable items were being discarded unnecessarily. Meanwhile, the second-hand market in Thailand was growing rapidly, but issues such as fraud, transaction difficulties, and lack of payment security discouraged many people from engaging in it.

Amidst these challenges, we asked ourselves: “Why isn't there a trusted second-hand marketplace that everyone in Thailand can use with confidence?”

This is why Buy N Sell was created.

Our goal is not just to provide a platform for buying and selling used goods. We want to create a safe, reliable trading environment where people can engage in sustainable consumption while also protecting the environment. To achieve this, we have built a secure and user-friendly system with:

A trusted trading environment

Buy N Sell prevents fraud through seller verification, secure payments, and a transparent review system. We will continue to strengthen security with AI-based fraud detection and enhanced protection features to ensure all users can trade with confidence.

A more convenient way to trade

We simplify personal transactions by addressing location coordination, delivery concerns, and complicated processes. Our features include scheduled meet-ups, integrated delivery services, and a one-click listing system, making buying and selling easier than ever.

Promoting sustainability and reducing waste

Thailand generates hundreds of thousands of tons of discarded electronics, furniture, and clothing each year. Many of these items are still valuable and can be reused, contributing to resource conservation and environmental protection. By encouraging second-hand trading, Buy N Sell aims to establish a culture of reuse, reduce carbon emissions, and promote eco-friendly consumption.

Supporting small businesses and local economies

Many small businesses in Thailand struggle with high fees and complex procedures on major e-commerce platforms. Buy N Sell provides affordable selling options, tailored advertising solutions, and business-friendly features to help them grow.

Expanding beyond Thailand

After establishing a successful model in Thailand, Buy N Sell aims to expand into Vietnam, Malaysia, Indonesia, and beyond, bringing a safe and efficient second-hand marketplace to more people across Southeast Asia.

We started in Thailand, but our vision extends far beyond.

Buy N Sell is not just a marketplace—it is a movement towards a smarter, more sustainable trading culture, built by and for the people of Thailand.

Join Buy N Sell and be part of a better way to trade, a better environment, and a better Thailand.

Our Mission

At Buy n Sell, our mission is to create a vibrant second-hand marketplace that empowers customers to connect with one another based on their geographical proximity, allowing them to discover and acquire the items they need from their local community. We believe in fostering a sense of community and sustainability by facilitating seamless transactions between buyers and sellers, ultimately enhancing the experience of finding unique, pre-loved goods right around the corner. By prioritizing location-based interactions, we strive to make it easier for individuals to access a diverse array of products while promoting responsible consumption and reducing waste in our society.

Our Purpose

At Buy and Sell, our purpose is to create a vibrant marketplace where customers can connect with each other to find exactly what they need. We understand that every buyer has unique wants, and every seller has something valuable to offer. Our app bridges the gap between these individuals, facilitating direct interactions that empower users to discover products within their local communities.

By focusing on location-based searches, we ensure that users can easily find items nearby, making the buying and selling process more convenient and efficient. Our goal is to foster a sense of community, enabling people to support one another while fulfilling their needs.

In essence, our purpose is to simplify the marketplace experience, encouraging connections, promoting sustainability through second-hand trading, and enhancing the way people shop locally. With Buy and Sell, you’re not just finding items; you’re engaging with your community.
