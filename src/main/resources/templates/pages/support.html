1. Introduction

At Buy N Sell (BNS), we are com

mitted to maintaining a safe, legal, and trustworthy marketplace for all users. To ensure compliance with Thai law and platform integrity, we provide clear account deletion procedures, both voluntary and company-initiated.

This policy outlines the steps for user-initiated account deletion and the circumstances under which Buy N Sell may permanently remove an account.

2. How to Delete Your Account

A. In-App Account Deletion

Users can delete their account directly within the Buy N Sell app by following these steps:

  • Open the Buy N Sell app.
  • Go to Settings > Account Settings.
  • Select rong>Delete My Account and follow the instructions.
  • Confirm the request. Once confirmed, the account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

B. Web-Based Account Deletion

If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion through our web portal:

  • Visit our Account Deletion Page.
  • Log in with your registered credentials.
  • Submit a Deletion Request Form with your account details.
  • You will receive an email confirmation. Follow the instructions to verify your request.

3. Account Deletion by the Company

A. Violations of Thai Law or Platform Policies

Your account may be deleted, with or without prior warning, if you engage in any of the following prohibited activities:

  • Illegal Transactions - Selling or purchasing items prohibited by Thai law.
  • Fraudulent Transactions - Engaging in deception, misrepresentation, or fraud against buyers or sellers.
  • Legal Violations - Conducting transactions that violate Thai law or encourage illegal activities.

The severity of the violation, as judged by the platform, will determine whether a warning is issued before deletion.

B. Other Grounds for Account Deletion

  • Extended Inactivity: Accounts inactive for 12 months or longer may be deleted after prior notice.
  • Multiple Policy Violations: Users repeatedly violating Buy N Sell's Terms of Service may be permanently removed.
  • Suspicious or Fraudulent Behavior: Accounts flagged for money laundering, scams, or fraud may be deleted without notice.

C. Permanent Ban from Buy N Sell

Once an account is permanently deleted by Buy N Sell, it cannot be restored. Users banned due to violations will be permanently blocked from registering again.

4. What Happens After Account Deletion?

  • Your profile, transaction history, and personal data will be permanently erased.
  • Any active listings, saved items, and messages will be deleted.
  • Some transactional data may be retained for legal and security purposes as per our Privacy Policy.
  • Deleted accounts cannot be recovered, and banned users cannot re-register.
